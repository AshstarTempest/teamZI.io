The Food Craving Dilemma

Many people struggle to decide what to eat when cravings strike. Despite having access to countless options, they often feel overwhelmed or uncertain about what will satisfy them. This indecision can lead to frustration, decision fatigue, or even settling for something unsatisfying. Our chatbot aims to solve this by asking targeted questions to quickly identify your preferences, helping you make the perfect food choice with minimal effort.

Our Idea

Our chatbot helps you satisfy your cravings by asking a few precise questions or chatting to understand your taste.

Quick Questions

A series of 5-6 quick questions to understand your preferences and suggest the best options.

Friendly Chat

Engages in a friendly conversation to figure out what you're craving in a more natural way.

How It Works

The chatbot interacts with APIs like Zomato or Swiggy to fetch food suggestions based on your preferences.

Step 1

Chatbot analyzes your cravings.

Step 2

Fetches food suggestions from delivery platforms.

Step 3

Refines options and helps you order.
